# Third-party boundary

- InsightFace Python code: MIT.
- ONNX Runtime: MIT.
- OpenCV: Apache-2.0.
- NumPy: BSD-3-Clause.

InsightFace upstream states that its supplied pretrained model packs are for
non-commercial research and directs users to contact InsightFace for separate
recognition-model licensing. This adapter contains no model weights, model
download helper or redistribution grant. Operators may instead supply their own
compatible model with independently established rights.
